# SatsFlow Compressed Pitch Deck

## Slide 1 - One-Liner
SatsFlow enables continuous Bitcoin-backed contributor payouts on Stacks.
Fund once, earnings accrue every second, recipients withdraw anytime.

## Slide 2 - Problem
Internet-native work is continuous, but payouts are still periodic.
- Contributors wait days or weeks for earnings already generated.
- Teams run manual payout operations.
- Cross-border payout timing and fees create friction.

## Slide 3 - Solution
SatsFlow turns contributor compensation into a live payment stream.
- Sender creates and funds a stream.
- Recipient sees real-time accrual in the UI.
- Recipient withdraws on demand via on-chain settlement.

## Slide 4 - Why Stacks + sBTC
- Stacks provides Bitcoin-aligned app infrastructure.
- sBTC enables Bitcoin-backed programmable payout rails.
- Clarity makes stream logic auditable and deterministic.

## Slide 5 - MVP (Hackathon Build)
- Single sender -> single recipient stream.
- Create stream + fund stream.
- Live accrual display.
- Recipient withdraw.
- Sender top-up and cancel with unaccrued refund.

## Slide 6 - Demo Flow
1. Connect sender wallet.
2. Create sBTC stream to contributor.
3. Switch recipient wallet and show live accrued amount.
4. Withdraw claimable value on demand.
5. Show updated balances and stream state.

## Slide 7 - Why This Wins
- Practical and differentiated: continuous Bitcoin-backed payouts.
- Real implementation depth: contract logic + working flow.
- Strong UX clarity: instant mental model for users and judges.

## Slide 8 - Users + Beachhead
Initial users:
- Stacks-native teams
- Open-source maintainer groups
- Creator collectives
- Grant-funded contributor networks

## Slide 9 - Bounty Fit
Primary fit: Most Innovative Use of sBTC.
SatsFlow uses sBTC as payment infrastructure, not passive collateral.
Secondary fit: USDCx path if integration is stable.

## Slide 10 - Close + Ask
SatsFlow makes compensation internet-native: continuous, transparent, and self-custodied.
Ask: support pilot intros to teams that pay contributors in recurring cycles.

"SatsFlow is a Stacks-native app for continuous Bitcoin-backed payouts.
